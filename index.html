About
Me

My name is Edgar Degas and I am a French Painter, sculptor, and printmaker born in Paris in 1834. My primary focus is impressionist paintings, of which I am famous.

I prefer to paint women of various types from singers, laborers, and street workers. My prefered range of media includes oil, etching, pastel, gouache, wax, and photography.
